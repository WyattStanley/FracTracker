1: Refinaries

This map displays Washington facilities that are regulated by the Washington State Department of Ecologys Spillss Program. These points display fixed shore-side facilities such as refinareis and refueling termianls. This includes facilites that transfer to or from tank vessels and pipelines. These facilites refine products such as Gas, Disel, Crude Oil, Motor Oil, Edible Oils and more. Refinary Data.

These points include refinareis that are used by Washingtons larges oil pipelines which include the Olympic pipeline, the Trans Mountain pipeline, the Yellowstone and Chevron pipeline, and the Northwest pipeline. These pipelines carry millions of gallons of crude oils, natural gas, and refined products thousands of miles accross Washington State. In the case of the Northwest pipeline, pipe circumference and commpressors are being updated to transport more oil accross states such as Washington, Oregon, and California who don't support the continuation or investment in the oil industry.

The maps below with go into more detail on how these pipelines effect the communities and environments of Washington State.
